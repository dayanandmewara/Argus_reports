Company Overview
What Anthem Does

Anthem Biosciences (est. 2006, Bangalore) is India's fastest-growing tech-driven CRDMO providing end-to-end drug lifecycle services — from first-in-class discovery to large-scale commercial manufacturing — exclusively for new molecules (NCE & NBE). Unlike API or generics manufacturers, Anthem captures premium value across the full drug development value chain.

Two revenue pillars: CRDMO Services (81.65% FY25 / ~83.5% FY26) covering small molecules, biologics, ADCs, RNAi, oligonucleotides, peptides; and Specialty Ingredients (18.35% / 16.5%) covering GLP-1, probiotics, enzymes, Vitamin K2, biosimilars, animal nutrition.
