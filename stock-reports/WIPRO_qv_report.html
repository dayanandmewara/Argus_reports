Opening Analyst Note — Series Classification

Category C — Mature Quality Compounder. Wipro Limited is India's third-largest IT services and consulting company with $10.5Bn annual revenue, 233,000+ employees across 65 countries, and a 78-year operating history. Its historic moat rested on client switching costs (deep multi-year enterprise integrations), scale (ability to absorb mega-deal complexity), and intangible assets (Capco financial consulting franchise, domain IP). ROIC at 15–17% consistently exceeds the 13% WACC, qualifying it as a narrow-to-moderate moat compounder.

The AI Overlay is unambiguously 🔴 AI THREAT. The IT Services sector table in this framework rates AI impact as THREAT — generative AI, agentic platforms, and code-generation tools are automating 20–35% of the work (ADM, testing, QA, basic support) that constitutes Wipro's largest revenue bucket. This is structural compression in billable hours, not a cyclical fluctuation. Wipro's revenue was flat in USD terms across FY24/FY25/FY26 ($10.5Bn each), and Q1 FY27 guidance is –2% to 0% CC — the weakest near-term signal.

The framework adaptation needed: apply 10% additional IV discount (AI THREAT) on all valuation methods; shorten investment horizon to 5–7yr vs standard 7–10yr for Cat C; widen MoS to 22–25%. Monitor quarterly for flywheel upgrade signal (currently FEATURE; upgrade to FORMING if cross-client AI data aggregation proven).

Moat Sources — Detailed Assessment
Switching Costs ★★★★★
Deep integration in client ERP, cloud, and BPO workflows. Multi-year contracts (3–5yr avg). Capco embeds in client C-suites in financial services. Re-tendering costs are genuinely high — data migration, retraining, integration risk. BUT: AI is lowering the switching barrier by abstracting integration complexity via API layers. Competitors can integrate faster with AI.
Scale Advantages ★★★★★
233,000+ employees, 65 countries. Can absorb $500Mn+ large deal complexity that smaller vendors cannot. India talent cost arbitrage. 62.1% offshore revenue — structural cost advantage. BUT: AI commoditises raw headcount. "We can staff 10,000 engineers" is a weakening moat as clients need fewer engineers with AI.
Intangible Assets ★★★★★
Capco financial consulting brand — top-tier in capital markets and banking transformation. Designit in design strategy. 15+ year enterprise relationships. Domain IP across BFSI, healthcare, manufacturing. DURABLE — consulting intangibles are AI-resistant; domain expertise amplified by AI rather than replaced.
Network Effects ★★★★★
Minimal. Each client engagement is largely independent. Wipro Intelligence platform has theoretical cross-client learning potential but unproven at scale. Contractual data restrictions prevent true network effect formation. WEAK competitive advantage source.
Efficient Scale ★★★★★
Global delivery model provides structural cost advantage vs. onshore-only competitors. India engineering cost base is 60–70% cheaper than US equivalents. AI may reduce but not eliminate this advantage — human-in-the-loop oversight still needed at scale.
Data Flywheel ★★★★★
Wipro processes vast enterprise data but cannot legally aggregate cross-client. No proven self-reinforcing AI improvement loop. Wipro Intelligence benefits from hyperscaler partner models improving globally. COSMETIC-to-FEATURE. Moat upgrade trigger NOT met.
