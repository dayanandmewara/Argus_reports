Valuation & Margin of Safety (HFC Framework — P/B Primary)

Primary Valuation Metric for HFCs: Price-to-Book Value (P/B) using the Gordon Growth Model: Justified P/B = (ROE − g) / (COE − g). For BHFL, the current P/B of 3.0x requires ROE of 18%+ to justify at COE 12% and g=10%. This is the crux — ROE today is 13% but CAR compression from 28% → 15% will mechanically drive ROE to 17-20% over 4-5 years. This is the bull thesis.
