Business Overview

Company Profile

JM Financial Limited (founded 1973, listed 1988) is India's premier independent investment bank and diversified financial services group headquartered in Mumbai. The group operates across four core segments: Integrated Investment Bank, Mortgage Lending, Alternative & Distressed Credit, and Platform AWS (Asset Management, Wealth Management, Securities). Present across 215 cities with 814 locations, it serves institutional, corporate, government, and high-net-worth clients.

Why This Stock Is In The Series

JM Financial is India's definitive ECM franchise — #1 in IPOs with 47% market share in FY24 and 80% share of India's top-10 IPOs by size. The FY2024 distressed-credit write-down (₹985 Cr exceptional) massively distorted earnings perception. With wholesale credit being wound down and the Platform AWS segment now doubling annually, this is a quality-franchise-at-value inflection — potentially one of the best fee-income financial stocks India has to offer if ROE recovery is confirmed.

Strategic Pivot — May 2024

The Defining Strategic Shift

In May 2024, the JM Financial board approved a landmark strategic pivot: exit on-balance-sheet wholesale credit and transition to a distribution-led, fee/commission model. The wholesale mortgage book was slashed from ₹4,900 Cr (Mar 2024) to ₹2,800 Cr (Mar 2025). Borrowings fell 29% YoY from ₹16,228 Cr to ₹11,507 Cr — the balance sheet is being deliberately deleveraged.

This is a textbook value-enhancing move: capital-heavy, NPA-prone wholesale credit → capital-light, recurring fee income from IB, wealth management, mutual funds. The ROE trough is likely FY2024–FY2025; FY2026 onwards should see structural ROE expansion toward 12–15% as the mix tilts toward fee businesses. The TTM PAT of ₹1,274 Cr is already showing the recovery's early results.
