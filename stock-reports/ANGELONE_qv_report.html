Company Overview
What Angel One Is

Founded 1996 (as Angel Broking). India's largest listed discount broker by client base (~37 million total; ~6.75 million NSE active). Listed on NSE/BSE since 2020. Flat ₹20/trade brokerage model. Pivoting from pure discount broker to full-stack fintech platform with broking, wealth management (Onyx), asset management, credit distribution, insurance, and fixed deposits — all on one app.

Why This Stock is in the Series

India's equity participation is at just 12% of population vs 50%+ in developed markets. Angel One is a direct play on India's financialization decade. SEBI's F&O reforms have compressed near-term earnings but structured a more sustainable industry — Angel One's platform model benefits from regulatory consolidation eliminating weaker players. The AI-driven personalization engine (ARQ Prime) is a developing competitive moat in a winner-take-most market.
