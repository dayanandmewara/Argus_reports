Business Overview

Jeena Sikho Lifecare Limited (JSLL), founded in 2009 by Acharya Manish Ji and headquartered in Zirakpur, Punjab, is India's largest organised clinical Ayurveda network operating under the 'Shuddhi' brand. As of Q3 FY26, JSLL operates 58 hospitals and 59 clinics/daycare centres across 23 states and 100+ cities with 2,290 operational beds (2,800 total capacity). The company runs a dual-engine model: Ayurveda Healthcare Services (IPD, OPD, Panchakarma, VOPD) and Ayurveda Healthcare Products (330+ SKUs under AYUSH compliance, ~85% gross margin). Services and products contribute approximately 49% and 51% of revenue respectively as of 9M FY26.
