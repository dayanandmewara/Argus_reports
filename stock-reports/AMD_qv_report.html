Why AMD — Why Now

AMD is the world's only credible scaled alternative to NVIDIA in AI accelerators. The company has undergone a structural transformation under Dr. Lisa Su — from a struggling x86 CPU challenger to a full-stack AI infrastructure provider. The Instinct MI series (MI300 → MI350 → MI450 → MI500) and EPYC CPU franchise now serve every major hyperscaler. The upcoming Helios rack-scale platform (MI450 GPUs + EPYC Venice CPUs + Pensando NICs) directly challenges NVIDIA's Grace Blackwell at the system level.

Key thesis: Agentic AI dramatically increases CPU demand alongside GPU demand — the server CPU TAM has been revised from $60B to >$120B by 2030. AMD captures both sides of this equation uniquely, making it structurally different from a pure GPU play.
