Business Overview OVERVIEW

Waaree Energies Limited (NSE: WAAREEENER) is India's largest solar photovoltaic module manufacturer with ~26 GW of installed module capacity and the single largest non-Chinese solar manufacturer globally. Founded in 1990 and pivoting to solar in 2007, the company operates five manufacturing facilities in Gujarat, with a new 3 GW module plant at Samakhiali commissioned in Q4 FY26 and India's first 10 GW integrated ingot-wafer facility under construction in Nagpur (₹6,200 Cr capex). The company is executing "Waaree 2.0" — a strategic transformation from a module manufacturer to a fully integrated clean energy provider spanning cells, modules, BESS, inverters, transformers, green hydrogen electrolysers, and power semiconductors (via Waaree Semicon acquisition). Revenue streams: Solar PV Modules (~88%), EPC Solutions (~8%), Power Generation (~4%).

Why This Stock is in the Series THESIS

Waaree sits at the epicentre of India's most important structural growth theme — the 500 GW renewable energy mission by 2030, requiring ~200 GW of additional solar. As the dominant domestic manufacturer (21% market share) and leading exporter (44% of India's solar exports pre-tariff), it has the scale, order book, and vertical integration runway that no domestic peer can match. The FY26 results — PAT doubling, EBITDA beating guidance — confirm execution quality. The stock's ~10% post-results decline reflects market disappointment with Q4 EBITDA margin compression (18.59% vs 23-25% prior quarters) and ongoing tariff uncertainty. This creates a framework question: does the business deserve BUY at the right price, or does the tariff + overcapacity + capex cycle risk argue for patience?
