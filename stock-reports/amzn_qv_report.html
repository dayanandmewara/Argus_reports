What Amazon Is

Amazon is a four-engine value machine: (1) AWS — the world's largest cloud platform, generating $150B+ annualized revenue at ~37% operating margin; (2) Advertising — a quietly dominant ~$65B high-margin business; (3) North America Retail — a $387B operating system for commerce with improving unit economics; and (4) International — the emerging markets optionality (~$143B revenue, approaching profitability).

What makes Amazon extraordinary is the flywheel across all four engines: Prime subscribers fuel advertising data; advertising data improves AWS's AI training sets; AWS capabilities lower Amazon's internal cost structure; lower costs attract more third-party sellers; more sellers improve Prime selection. This is a self-compounding value creation engine.

Why This Stock Now

Amazon is in the midst of a historic AI infrastructure investment cycle — $125B+ capex in FY2025, likely $150B+ in FY2026. This suppresses near-term FCF (just $7.7B in FY2025) but is building an unassailable AI position for 2028-2032.

The April 2026 Anthropic $100B AWS commitment (Anthropic commits to $100B+ AWS spending over 10 years, Amazon invests $25B in Anthropic) is a landmark strategic lock-in. AWS Trainium3 coming online in 2026 and AWS running 100,000+ Bedrock customers makes this the most compelling AI infrastructure story outside of Nvidia.

Challenge: At $270, AMZN trades ~6-10% above our AI-adjusted Base IV ($245). This is WATCH territory — exceptional quality, slightly elevated entry.
