COMPANY OVERVIEW
CATEGORY C · MATURE QUALITY COMPOUNDER · NSE: POLYCAB
Business in One Sentence
Polycab India is India's #1 wires & cables manufacturer by revenue and market share (~26% organized domestic), expanding into FMEG consumer electricals, with a multi-decade brand moat built on quality, safety, and distribution scale across 4,000+ distributors and 200,000+ retail touchpoints.
Why in the QV Series?
Polycab sits at the intersection of every major India structural theme — government infrastructure capex, power sector transformation, real estate boom, EV ecosystem, and data center proliferation. As a Category C mature compounder with ROCE ~30%, near-zero debt, and 26% revenue CAGR over 5 years, it earns a place in the series. The challenge: Is the market pricing in too much perfection?

4. FMEG margin dilution — fans business underperforming

5. Promoter stake declining (-4.7% over 3 years)

Standard Category C framework applies (WACC 12–13%, terminal P/E 20–22×). AI Overlay = NEUTRAL; company uses AI for demand sensing and logistics but this does not structurally change the competitive position. W&C is a physically manufactured product — AI disruption risk is LOW. No DCF adjustments needed for AI.
