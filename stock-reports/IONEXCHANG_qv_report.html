🏭 Business Overview WATER & ENVIRONMENT
Company Description

Ion Exchange (India) Limited, founded in 1964, is India's largest integrated water and environment management company. Headquartered in Mumbai, it offers end-to-end solutions across the full water treatment spectrum — pre-treatment, process water, wastewater, zero liquid discharge (ZLD), desalination, sewage treatment, and packaged drinking water.

The company operates through three distinct business segments: Engineering (EPC water treatment plants — largest revenue contributor), Chemicals (ion exchange resins, speciality chemicals — highest-margin segment), and Consumer Products (ZeroB brand home water purifiers — strategically important, currently loss-making).

VERIFIED The company has 8 manufacturing/assembly facilities in India, 1 in Portugal, and assembly operations in UAE, Indonesia, Bangladesh, and Saudi Arabia. Over 2,000 treatment plants supplied globally, many World Bank-funded.
