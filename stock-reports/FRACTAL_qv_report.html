AI DISRUPTION RISK TO FRACTAL'S OWN MOAT
The Paradox: Fractal benefits from enterprise AI adoption — but the same AI wave could eventually reduce the need for human AI consultants if foundation models become powerful enough. This is the central long-term risk.

AI Threat Dimensions

  • AI coding tools (Cursor, Copilot): Could reduce data engineering headcount required for delivery
  • GenAI for analytics: Commoditises lower-complexity analytics work in 3–5 years
  • Timeline: Medium-term for simple work; high-complexity AI strategy is protected longer

Fractal's Defense

  • Already building the AI that replaces old work: Fractal IS the AI tools company
  • Moving up the value chain: Outcome-based contracts, C-suite advisory, Fractal Alpha
  • If AI reduces delivery cost, margins IMPROVE: The risk becomes an opportunity if executed
Moat Resilience: 6.5/10 — Strong enough for a 5-year horizon. Switching cost + talent brand moats are durable. Primary risk: commoditisation of lower-complexity analytics in 3–5 years. Fractal's strategic response (Cogentiq, outcome-based pricing) is correct, but execution is the uncertainty.
