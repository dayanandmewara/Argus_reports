Business Overview
What Micron Does

Micron Technology (NASDAQ: MU) designs, manufactures, and sells memory and storage products globally. It operates through four segments: Cloud Memory (CMBU), Core Data Center (CDBU), Mobile & Client (MCBU), and Automotive & Embedded (AEBU).

Products span DRAM, NAND, NOR flash, SSDs, and the strategically critical High-Bandwidth Memory (HBM) — 3D-stacked DRAM delivering 5-10× the bandwidth of conventional DRAM, essential for AI GPU accelerators.

Data center = 56% of FY2025 revenue ($20.75B, +137% YoY). VERIFIED
