Business Overview

Amagi (est. 2008, Bengaluru) is a cloud-native SaaS platform providing end-to-end "glass-to-glass" infrastructure for the global media and entertainment industry — from content ingest to broadcast playout to FAST distribution to programmatic CTV monetization.

The company operates across three revenue segments: Cloud Modernization (replacing legacy broadcast hardware), Streaming Unification (managing multi-platform distribution), and Monetization & Marketplace (THUNDERSTORM SSAI ad insertion, Amagi ADS PLUS programmatic marketplace). Streaming Unification is the largest and fastest-growing segment (~52.86% of H1 FY26 revenue).

Listed on BSE & NSE in January 2026 (IPO ₹361/share). Serves 45 of the top 50 global media companies. Revenue 67%+ from the US. Debt-free with IPO cash reserves.

★★★★

Media companies building entire broadcast/streaming infrastructure on Amagi's platform face enormous switching costs — re-training staff, rebuilding channel workflows, migrating content libraries, and re-establishing distributor connections. The "glass-to-glass" nature means Amagi touches every step of production-to-audience. NRR of 126.9% empirically validates these switching costs. VERIFIED

★★★★
