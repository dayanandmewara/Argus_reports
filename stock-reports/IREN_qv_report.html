Business Overview
IREN is a vertically integrated AI Cloud and Bitcoin mining data center company, incorporated in Australia and listed on NASDAQ.
Founded 2018 by Daniel and William Roberts; operates renewable-powered data centers in Texas, British Columbia (Canada), Spain (via Nostrum, pending), and APAC pipeline.
Deliberate pivot underway: ASICs being decommissioned and replaced with NVIDIA GPU clusters for AI training and inference workloads.
Three revenue verticals: (1) Bitcoin Mining, (2) AI Cloud Services, (3) AI Data Centers (colocation/hyperscale). Mining still dominant in FY25 but AI Cloud will dominate by FY27.
Series Rationale — Why IREN?
Power scarcity as structural moat: Grid-connected power in renewable-rich regions is one of the scarcest assets in the AI economy. IREN has 4.5GW+ secured.
Extraordinary validation: When Microsoft signs $9.7B and NVIDIA signs $3.4B + offers $2.1B equity option, the speculative infrastructure thesis becomes contracted reality.
Vertical integration bet: Mirantis acquisition ($625M, pending) moves IREN from commodity compute to managed AI cloud with software orchestration (k0rdent platform).
Key risk: Execution on 140,000 GPUs by end 2026 is extremely ambitious. Revenue is declining QoQ in transition. Heavy dilution from convertibles, Mirantis, and NVIDIA option.
