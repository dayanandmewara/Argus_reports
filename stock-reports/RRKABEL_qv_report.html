🏢 Business Overview
Company Profile

R R Kabel Limited (est. 1999) is a leading player in the Indian Fast-Moving Electrical Goods (FMEG) and Wires & Cables (W&C) space. Under its retail brand "RR Kabel", it ranks as the third-largest organized manufacturer of wires and cables in India. The company operates two state-of-the-art DSIR-approved manufacturing facilities in Waghodia (Gujarat) and Silvassa, catering to domestic and international markets. Wires & Cables comprise ~88% of consolidated revenue, while the consumer FMEG business (fans, lighting, switchgears) represents ~12% and is scaling rapidly toward operational breakeven. VERIFIED
