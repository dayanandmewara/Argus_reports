Business Overview

CleanMax Enviro Energy Solutions is India's largest pure-play C&I (Commercial & Industrial) renewable energy platform. Founded in 2011 by Kuldeep Jain (ex-McKinsey Global Partner, IIM Ahmedabad), backed by Brookfield Asset Management. The company operates a Build-Own-Operate (OPEX) model providing solar, wind, and hybrid renewable energy to 550+ corporate clients across 23+ Indian states and international markets (UAE, Bahrain, Thailand).
