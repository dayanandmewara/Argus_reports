Business Overview

Gem Aromatics Limited (est. 1997, listed Aug 2025) is India's leading specialty ingredients manufacturer focused on essential oils, aroma chemicals, and value-added derivatives. The company is built around two primary chemistries: Mint (69% of FY25 revenue) and Clove (19%). It holds dominant market positions — 65% share in Eugenol, 58% in Eucalyptus Oil, and is one of India's largest processors of DMO and Piperita oil by volume. Its 70-product portfolio serves oral care (Colgate-Palmolive, Dabur, Patanjali), flavours & fragrances (Symrise, SH Kelkar), nutraceuticals, and personal care across 225 domestic and 44 international customers in 18 countries.
