Business Overview
Ceigall India Limited — 23-yr-old infrastructure EPC company founded in Ludhiana 2002 by Ramneek Sehgal. Specialises in complex structural works: elevated roads, flyovers, tunnels, metro viaducts, bridges, expressways, airport runways across 12+ states. Listed NSE/BSE Aug 2024 at ₹401 IPO price (current ₹372 = −7.2%).
