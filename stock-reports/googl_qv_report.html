Special Context: Why This Stock Now

1. AI VINDICATION: After years of "Google is losing search to ChatGPT" narratives, Q1 2026 proved the opposite — Search queries are at all-time highs, revenue growing 19%, and Google's info retrieval market share expanded from 70% to 75% as AI rivals' share shrank (Evercore ISI, March 2026). Gemini went from 7M MAU in Q4 2023 to 750M MAU in Q4 2025 — a 107× increase powered by Google's ecosystem distribution.

2. CLOUD BREAKOUT: Google Cloud grew 63% in Q1 2026 — faster than AWS or Azure — and Pichai said the company is "compute constrained" and cloud revenue would have been higher if supply was available. The $460B backlog is the largest in GCP's history, nearly doubling QoQ. This is a fundamental shift.

3. ANTITRUST RESOLVED (MOSTLY): Judge Mehta ruled September 2025: no Chrome or Android sale. Behavioral remedies (no exclusive default contracts; data sharing). DOJ appealing, but structural breakup risk is very low. Loss of ~$20B Apple default deal was expected — redirecting that cash toward Gemini/Cloud investment is arguably value-accretive.

4. THE CAPEX QUESTION: The single most important debate. $180–190B capex in 2026 (and rising in 2027) will crush FCF to ~$20B. Management says "We are compute constrained" — this is capex to meet demand, not to create demand. If Cloud backlog converts at 50%+ in 24 months ($230B+ of secured revenue), the ROI is exceptional. If it doesn't, this is a capital destruction event.
