Business Overview

Incorporated 1997, Gem Aromatics manufactures and exports essential oils, aroma chemicals and value-added derivatives — mint/menthol, clove/eugenol, eucalyptus, and phenol (anethole) product lines — serving oral care, cosmetics, nutraceuticals, pharma, wellness and personal-care customers domestically and in the US, Europe, Brazil, China and Singapore. VERIFIED

The company listed on NSE/BSE on 26 Aug 2025 at ₹325/share (₹451cr IPO, ₹175cr fresh issue + ₹276cr OFS), using proceeds largely to repay debt. VERIFIED It is now commissioning a ₹270cr greenfield facility at Dahej (Krystal Ingredients subsidiary) intended to triple installed capacity to ~16,000 MTPA and pivot the mix toward higher-value cooling agents, clove/eugenol derivatives and new molecules (GEM Cool 5, Safranal). Management guides ₹1,050–1,100cr revenue by FY28 at 16–18% EBITDA margin — roughly 2.3x current TTM revenue. ESTIMATED — forward guidance, unverified track record
