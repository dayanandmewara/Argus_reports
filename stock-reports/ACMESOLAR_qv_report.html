Business Overview
What ACME Solar Does

ACME Solar Holdings Limited is one of India's largest pure-play renewable energy IPPs. Listed Nov 2024, it develops, builds, owns, and operates utility-scale solar, wind, hybrid, BESS, and Firm & Dispatchable Renewable Energy (FDRE) projects across 10 states. Revenue flows exclusively from 25-year PPAs with central and state entities — SECI, NTPC, NHPC, DISCOMs. ACME is differentiated by in-house EPC and O&M, and is among India's earliest FDRE operators capable of delivering round-the-clock dispatchable clean power.

Economic Moat Analysis
PPA CONTRACT LOCK-IN
★★★★☆
6,570 MW of signed PPAs with SECI, NHPC, NTPC at fixed tariffs (₹2.5–6.28/unit) for 25 years. Contracts with central counterparties (86% of portfolio) carry sovereign-equivalent payment security. Once a PPA is signed at competitive tariff, it cannot be replicated — genuine switching-cost analog. However, no tariff upside exists post-signing.
Macro Stress: HOLDS — Central PPAs are recession-proof
LAND + ISTS CONNECTIVITY
★★★☆☆
ISTS grid connectivity in Rajasthan, AP, Gujarat, MP is increasingly scarce — CERC queues are 3–5 years. Competitors cannot easily replicate ACME's grid positions. Large land parcels in solar-rich zones = unbooked asset not on balance sheet. First-mover advantage in high-irradiation ISTS corridors.
Macro Stress: HOLDS — Physical scarcity strengthens in a capacity boom
IN-HOUSE EPC & O&M
★★★☆☆
Internal EPC yields ~5–8% lower project cost vs third-party. Enabled 1,200 MW single-day commissioning in Q3 FY25. MTBF/MTTR optimisation drives 25.6% CUF (above sector avg). O&M cost ~₹3–4 L/MW/yr vs ₹5–7 L/MW/yr outsourced. Provides direct quality control over the asset portfolio.
Macro Stress: HOLDS — Cost advantage compounds at scale
FDRE / BESS FIRST-MOVER
★★★☆☆
Among India's first IPPs to operationalise commercial BESS (948 MWh live, 2.3 GWh by Dec 2026, 17 GWh pipeline). FDRE tariffs ₹5–6.3/unit vs ₹2.5–3.5/unit solar-only = premium moat. Recent SECI FDRE at ₹6.28/unit. However, replicable by Adani Green, Greenko in 2–3 years.
Macro Stress: STRENGTHENS — Grid reliability premium will grow as intermittent capacity scales
