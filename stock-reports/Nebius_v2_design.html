THE YANDEX REBIRTH — One of Tech's Most Dramatic Corporate Reinventions

Nebius is not a startup. It is the reborn non-Russian assets of Yandex — Russia's largest internet company co-founded in 1997 by CEO Arkady Volozh. After Russia's Ukraine invasion, Yandex's Russian assets (search, maps, taxi) were sold to a domestic consortium for ~$5B. The remaining Western assets — AI cloud, Toloka (data labeling), Avride (autonomous vehicles), TripleTen (edtech) — were reconstituted as Nebius Group N.V. on NASDAQ. EU sanctions on Volozh were lifted. This is a genuine clean-break, not a rebranding exercise. Every asset and employee is Western-domiciled.
